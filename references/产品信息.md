### X9 — Clean, Light & Free

&#x20;

Born in the heart of America’s Appalachian region, X9 is a modern brand dedicated to creating high-quality, lightweight, and comfortable disposable care products for every stage of life — for every member of your family.

The X9 brand philosophy: “Clean, Light & Free” which reflects our mission to bring a new sense of freedom to personal care. We believe that comfort, confidence, and cleanliness should be effortless. From feminine pads and period underwear to adult diapers, maternity pads, baby training pants, and pet pads, each product is carefully engineered for superior absorbency, breathability, and softness.

X9 stands for a better life, more than just protection.
